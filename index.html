Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is similiar but in a sense different than Graceful Degradation. Progressive Enhancement focuses on content more so than the browser contrary to Graceful Degradation. It strives to deliver a consistent level of content and functionality to all users regardless of platform or browser, but of course, the best experience will only be accessible to users on newer browsers as thatâ€™s the only platforms it can be available to (kind of like imessages functionality on a new iPhone vs an old. Progressive Enhancement is divided into three layers first the core and the simplest HTML, then the filling that coats the core which is CSS, and finally the shell (outer layer or presentation) which is Java-Script.
